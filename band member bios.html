Meet Spiderlily!

Emily: Vocals, Rhythm Guitar

Emily is the lead singer and rhythm guitarist for Spiderlily. She has a passion for all things art, and loves to mix mediums with visual experession and music. She loves wearing fun clothes and makeup on stage, and writing music that speaks to her experiences and important issues. Her favorite genre to write is punk rock, but she loves to experiment with different sounds and genres.
